OPTIMIZATIONS:

	GAME ENGINE:
	Support culling distances for light sources and mesh renderers

	RENDERING ENGINE:
	Cull lights if distance to camera is > farclip + intensity*13


FEATURES:
	SIMPLE LIGHT SOURCES:
		Attribute: double softness [0,1] ->
			the higher, the less will the dot factor (angle of the face) matter for its brightness.
			Value 0 means: faces that face away get no light at all
			Value 1 means: faces that face away are just as bright as ones that face directly towards the light source
